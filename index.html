Common Questions (No Judgment!)

I'm a complete beginner - is this really for me?
Absolutely! 90% of my clients are complete beginners. In fact, I just started my business because I'm passionate about helping women who feel exactly like you do right now. I remember being terrified of the weight room just a few years ago, so I know exactly how to help you feel comfortable and confident from day one.
What happens in the free consultation?
It's just a friendly chat! We'll talk about your goals (no matter how big or small), I'll answer all your questions, and we'll figure out if we're a good fit. No pressure, no judgment - just two people talking about fitness. It's over Zoom or phone, whatever you prefer!
I'm worried about bulking up - will lifting make me look masculine?
This is my most common question! Short answer: no. Building significant muscle mass requires specific training, nutrition, and often years of dedicated work. My programs focus on building lean, functional strength that makes you feel powerful and confident in your body.
What if I need to cancel or reschedule?
Life happens! Just give me 24 hours notice to reschedule in-person sessions. For online coaching, we work around YOUR schedule. No strict contracts - we can always adjust as needed.
Do you work with people who have injuries or limitations?
Yes! I work with many clients who have past injuries or physical limitations. We'll discuss any concerns during your consultation and I'll adapt exercises to work for YOUR body. Your safety and comfort always come first.
