Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in the free consultation?
Your free 30-minute consultation includes a fitness assessment, goal setting session, and a customized plan overview. We'll also discuss which training option best fits your lifestyle and budget.
Do you provide meal plans?
Yes! Both programs include nutrition guidance. Online coaching includes a comprehensive meal planning guide, while in-person clients receive personalized nutrition consultations.
What equipment do I need for online coaching?
Programs are customized based on your available equipment - from full gym access to home workouts with minimal equipment. I'll adapt the plan to what you have.
What's your cancellation policy?
In-person sessions require 24-hour notice for rescheduling. Online coaching operates on monthly billing with 30-day notice for cancellation. No long-term contracts required.
How do you track progress?
We use a combination of measurements, progress photos, strength benchmarks, and regular check-ins. Online clients use a dedicated app for easy tracking and communication.
